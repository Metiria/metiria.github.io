General information

Metiria is a band from eastern Sicily blending prog and pop elements together with heavy influences derived from modern metal. The band is currently composed by:

  • Aurora Amas (Vocals),
  • Samuele Mangiaratti (Drums),
  • Lorenzo Ragusa (Bass),
  • Angelo Li Rosi (Keyboards) and
  • Arturo Caflisch (Guitar).

Music style

The stylistic signature of the musical group is based on the balance between power and sound research: eight and twelve string guitars, six string basses, acoustic and electronic percussions, pianos and synths interweave with the intense and full-bodied voice of the front-woman. Extreme genres’ complex rhythms dialogue with wide melodies and harmonies ranging from classical counterpoint to contemporary modal music. The multifaceted identity of the band, result of the different musical backgrounds of its members, encompasses pop-rock, classical music, jazz, funk and metal.

Beginnig as a local project

The project takes its form and entirety in 2024 in Catania with the debut of Metiria in the setting of the Benedictine Monastery [link to Tour – Disummer 2024/10/05], but its birth dates back to the first writing sessions held right away in a small studio of their own. “The office” as they like to call it, is a simple but essential space built with consistency and passion by musicians, hosting frequent late-night jam sessions, technological experimentations and long-lasting hours of musical exchanges shaping the group’s identity up to present days. At the beginning of the band’s career, only a few stages had been tread: some local contests and festivals [link to Tour – Castel Rock 2025/06/01], a few live performances in small theaters [link to Tour – Teatro Coppola 2025/04/09] , and other minor gigs often providing the band with just expense reimbursements. Despite this, the band’s vision was already clear and, in on year’s time, the first national shows in Basilicata [link to Tour – Promuovi la tua musica 2024/12/21] and Calabria have been already put on.

Dawn of national and international success

The turning point came with the band’s participation to the Sanremo Rock contest. After being selected for the regional phase at first, and for the interregional round [link to Tour – Sanremo Rock Selections – 2025/08/01-02] subsequently, Metiria’s song Sorrow [link to Releases – Singles – Sorrow] reached the national grand final [link to Tour – Sanremo Rock Finals – 2025/09/13] which took place in the world-famous “Teatro Ariston” winning the third overall place [link to News – La Sicilia] of the renowned original music competition. This result marks the second era of the project arousing new interest and medial resonance for the Sicilian formation. Thanks to this experience the band is notified of the possibility to take part to the San Marino Song Contest. Metiria decides to catch this opportunity without any hesitation. Between more than 800 bands who applied for the Castings [link to Tour – Dreaming San Marino Casting – 2026/02/01], the band is included in the list of the first 240 gaining the access to the Stage & Live Academy [link to Tour – Dreaming San Marino Stage & Live academy], competing with its new song Attention seeker [link to Releases – Singles – Sorrow] with numerous national and international artists. Today the group made the top being selected for the first semifinal [link to Tour – San Marino Semifinals 2026/03/04], being part of the first 20 [link to News – La Sicilia] of the total of 40 artists running for a place in the grand final.

Present vision & future goals

The journey, built from the ground in a small and independent studio in the heart of Catania, has now grown step by step thanks to constant discipline and research merged with a precise artistic vision: blending creative technique and emotional intensity in a contemporary and recognisable language. This artist’s conception leads the current production of the band’s firts album [lik to Releases] soon available on all streaming platforms [link to Follow – Streaming platfforms] .
