Aurora Amas

Vocals

Catania, Italy

Music Education

Aurora Amas (born in Catania, June 5, 2001) began her vocal journey at the age of ten, initially performing in school choirs before transitioning to musical theatre ensembles at sixteen.

At nineteen, she commenced formal studies in Rock Vocals under specialized vocal coach Pietro Praticò, focusing on advanced technique, breath control, and stylistic versatility.

At twenty-four, driven by a passion for theatre and a desire to explore diverse sonic landscapes, she further expanded her repertoire by studying Opera under the guidance of Massimo Bellini’s Theater Choir Soprano Ester Ventura in Catania.

Bands and Projects

Her professional live career took shape in 2020 with the project 7UP, an alternative/rock ensemble consisting of seven musicians.

She subsequently performed with pop rock bands Paradigma and Numa Six, with whom she recorded her first original single, Unbended. Until 2025, she was also active in the project Chiaro di Luna, dedicated to 1980s pop.

Alongside numerous collaborations as a session vocalist, Aurora Amas is now the front woman of prog band Metiria as well lead voice for Songbirds, a duo performing at five-star resorts.

Inspirations

Her artistic identity is deeply rooted in Metal music. She is inspired by the intense textures and unique timbres of artists such as Serj Tankian (System of a Down), Einar Solberg (Leprous), Amy Lee (Evanescence), Eddie Vedder (Pearl Jam), M. Shadows (Avenged Sevenfold), Tarja Turunen (Nightwish), Dolores O'Riordan (The Cranberries), Mikael Åkerfeldt (Opeth), and Maynard James Keenan (Tool).

Style

Aurora Amas is fascinated by rich, deep vocal timbres, symphonic metal, and complex harmonic architectures.

She exhibits a meticulous approach to sound design and vocal layering, demonstrating an almost obsessive attention to detail regarding harmonies: she confirms that she isn’t satisfied unless she adds at least twenty tracks of choral layers for each song.

Other Relevant Information

A dedicated enthusiast of international culture, she is multilingual, speaking Italian, English, French, and Spanish.

In 2024, she earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Economics and Management and is currently pursuing a Master’s degree in the same discipline.
